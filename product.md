# Product Brief

## What we sell
An AI helpdesk chatbot powered by RAG that answers questions about your company, product, and training materials—helping both your team and your customers get instant, accurate answers.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Small to mid-sized companies (20–500 employees), especially fast-growing teams
- Industries: SaaS, startups, e-commerce, and support-heavy businesses
- Typical buyer persona: Head of Support, VP Operations, Founder, Head of Growth, or Customer Success Lead

## Pain points we solve
- Customers asking repetitive product questions before buying or while using the product
- Support teams overwhelmed with the same tickets and FAQs
- Internal knowledge scattered across docs, Notion, Slack, help centers, etc.
- Slow onboarding for both employees and customers

## Core value propositions
- One AI that serves both internal teams and customers with accurate, context-aware answers
- Reduces support tickets and deflects repetitive customer questions
- Improves conversion by helping prospects get answers instantly
- Speeds up onboarding and makes knowledge easily accessible

## Proof points
Recently, we built an AI chatbot solution for Internet2 and an AI procurement workflow for UCSD, so we’ve seen how this kind of automation can work in practice for organizations with complex or high-volume requests. Additionally, I worked as an AI forward deployed engineer at Amazon to developing automated workflows for various customers.

## Tone for outbound
- Casual, founder-to-founder
- Clear and slightly opinionated
- Focused on real pain (support overload, scattered knowledge)
- No corporate jargon
- Hyper personalized based on person I am selling to
